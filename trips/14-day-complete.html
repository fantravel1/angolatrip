Why 14 days?

Angola deserves time. Driving north to south in 7 days is possible but rushed. 14 days lets you:

✓ Breathe

No 8-hour drives every day. Explore each city properly. Talk to locals. Sit with views.

✓ Experience Seasons

Each region has different rhythms. Coast beaches, plateau cool, desert heat. Time to adjust.

✓ Go Deep

Waterfalls, viewpoints, hidden towns, music venues. Can't see it all in passing.

✓ Build Confidence

Longer immersion = better understanding of culture, logistics, roads, people.

North to South Itinerary

Days 1–3: Luanda

Urban immersion. Nightlife, markets, malls, waterfront. Get your Angola bearings.

Days 4–5: Kwanza Region

Sumbe beaches, Catumbela coast, fishing villages. Slow transition south.

Days 6–7: Benguela

Colonial town, calm beaches, fresh seafood. Cultural depth before the plateau.

Days 8–9: Plateau & Lubango

Huambo markets, Kalandula Falls, Miradouro da Lua, Lubango mountain city.

Days 10–12: Southern Coast

Namibe, Praia do Tanda, Iona desert. Desert-ocean fusion, fishing culture.

Days 13–14: Return or Relax

Return to Luanda or add extra days for deeper exploration based on interests.
