Desert travel tips

Sunscreen is non-negotiable (intense sun)

Water, water, water (dehydration sneaks)

Tank before leaving main towns

Sand roads can become impassable in rainy season

Bring binoculars (wildlife viewing)

Sunset timing = best photography window

Night skies are phenomenal (stars unfiltered)

Ask locals about current road conditions

4WD helpful but not always required

Carry extra fuel in containers
