Major coastal towns

Luanda

The Capital Coast

  • Urban beach culture, nightlife, restaurants
  • Mausoleum, Marginal promenade, beach clubs
  • Arrival point for most travelers
  • Best for: Culture, nightlife, urban exploration

Sumbe

Fishing Town Charm

  • Working harbor, fishing boats, genuine atmosphere
  • Fresh seafood, local bars, quiet beaches
  • Great first night south of Luanda
  • Best for: Authentic coastal experience

Benguela

Colonial Calm

  • Portuguese colonial architecture, relaxed pace
  • Beach walks, good restaurants, cool breezes
  • Perfect mid-trip rest point
  • Best for: Slowing down, cultural immersion

Namibe

Desert Meets Ocean

  • Unique red dunes transition to Atlantic
  • Fishing culture, isolated beauty, unspoiled beaches
  • Endpoint of most southern routes
  • Best for: Unique landscape, solitude
